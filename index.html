    Lab Booking Summary

    Book the DEXLab & Equipment

    The lab room and equipment must be booked separately via Resource Booker. Submit your bookings at least 2 days in advance so the DEXLab Manager can approve them.

    As a Master thesis student, ask your supervisor to make the booking for you if you don't have access.

    As a DEXLab TIP student, you can book the lab and equipment yourself.
