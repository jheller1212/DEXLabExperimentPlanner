This tool helps you plan your experiment from start to finish. Select your role and block period, and you'll get a personalised timeline with all key deadlines — SONA registration, ethics, lab booking, and more — including warnings for Dutch public holidays. You can check off tasks as you go, export milestones to your calendar, and share your plan via a link.
